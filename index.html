Poosheck

Finally a solution for all your cute-weather needs.

Poosheck is happy to see you

Look at him wagging his tail! He's so happy to be in your phone! He was originally created by Ryan Rumbolt. He's been through a couple of changes and now he likes to talk about the weather.

He's so cute he doesn't need to sleep.

Did you know that dogs as cute as this one do not need to sleep? They are up all night in order to tell you what the current weather is. And to wag their tail at you. Both at the same time! Wow!

Poosheck is open source

Which means you can make him better if you want to! Just go to this GitHub page and feel free to add your suggestions, log issues or even fix stuff. But don't try to make him cuter. We both know that's not humanly possible.

If you like this app maybe you'll like some other stuff I do as well :)
